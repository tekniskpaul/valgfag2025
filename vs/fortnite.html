Fortnite

About Fortnite

Genre: Battle Royale, Shooter

Release Year: 2017

Description: Fortnite is an online multiplayer battle royale game where 100 players fight to be the last one standing. Players can build structures, gather resources, and use various weapons to outlast other players on a shrinking map. It is known for its vibrant, cartoonish art style and frequent in-game events.

Key Features

  • Fast-paced battle royale gameplay
  • Building mechanics for structures and defenses
  • Regular updates and in-game events
  • Cross-platform play between PC, consoles, and mobile

Game Modes

  • Battle Royale: 100-player free-for-all battle until one player remains.
  • Creative: Build your own world and share it with others.
  • Save the World: A cooperative PvE mode where you fight off zombies and build defenses.
